Olympia, WA

Nestled at the southern tip of the Puget Sound, Olympia, Washington, serves as the charming capital of the state, blending a vibrant urban vibe with lush natural surroundings, featuring a grand Capitol building, bustling farmers market, and vibrant downtown that highlight its community spirit and Pacific Northwest allure.

Quick Facts

  • Population: 55,700
  • Established in 1850
  • It is a suburban city in Western Washington
  • Median Income of Olympia is $40,600 while the state's median income is $44,586
