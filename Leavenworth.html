Leavenworth, WA

Leavenworth, WA, is a picturesque Bavarian-themed village located in the heart of the Cascade Mountains. This enchanting town, with its charming alpine architecture, transports visitors to a European wonderland right in the Pacific Northwest. Leavenworth is renowned for its vibrant seasonal festivals, including Oktoberfest and the magical Christmas Lighting Festival, which draw visitors from all over. The town also offers a plethora of outdoor activities year-round, such as hiking, skiing, river rafting, and wine tasting, making it a perfect destination for both adventure enthusiasts and those seeking a cozy, cultural escape.

Quick Facts

  • Population: 2422
  • Established in 1885
  • It is a small rural city in Central Washington
  • Median Income of Leavenworth is $37,500 while the state's median income is $44,586
