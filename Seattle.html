Seattle, WA

Seattle, nestled in the Pacific Northwest of the United States, is a vibrant city renowned for its blend of natural beauty and urban sophistication. Surrounded by water, mountains, and evergreen forests, Seattle offers a picturesque backdrop that captivates visitors and locals alike. Known as the Emerald City, it boasts iconic landmarks such as the futuristic Space Needle and bustling Pike Place Market, where fresh seafood and local crafts abound.

Quick Facts

  • Population: 749,000
  • Established in 1851
  • It is the largest and most well known city in Washington State.
  • Median Income of Seattle is $63,157 while the state's median income is $44,586
